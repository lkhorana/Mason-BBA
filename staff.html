Transfer credit process — staff reference
1

Student submits request via Microsoft Form

They include their institution, course code, and syllabus link. BBA office reviews it first.

2a

Already on file (within 3 years)

Check this database. Email the student the decision directly. No faculty review needed.

2b

Not on file — initiate faculty review

Forward the syllabus to the relevant W&M department head. Follow up in 2–4 weeks.

3

Record the decision

Add or update the row in the Google Sheet transfer_credits tab. Set the expires date to 3 years from today. Paste the student's syllabus URL into the notes column.
