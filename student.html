How the transfer credit process works
1

Submit a request

Use the official Microsoft Form and attach your course syllabus. The BBA office reviews it first.

2a

Decision already on file

If reviewed within the last 3 years, the BBA office emails you directly with the outcome.

2b

New faculty review needed

Your syllabus is sent to the relevant W&M department head. Allow 2–4 weeks for a response.

3

Decision recorded

The outcome is added to the BBA database and shared with the Registrar and Advising Center.
